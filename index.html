Welcome to Our Company

Ansar Al Madeena Technical Contracting is a MEP based contracting company that majorly deals with the mechanical works in this field. AMTCO is now recognized as the leading sub-contractor in this field in UAE. The scope of activities are majorly all the mechanical equipment installation, chilled water piping, ducting, cladding, steel structure fabrication with installation and welding, pressure testing and flushing of system.

The company offers a 24/7 services according to the client demands. AMTCO is a fully registered and approved company according to the laws of UAE. Due to its round capability and high standards of working, the company is involved in many notable projects all around UAE.

We have accomplished the projects as sub-contractor to many reputed and multi-national companies in UAE.
